SAVINGS ACCOUNT

It is an essential service of the banking institution. The account holder gets the security of his deposit and civil interest on the money. Savings account of Sahyog Multi State Credit Co-op. Society Ltd. can be started with minimum Rs.100/- (Normal Savings Account) & Rs.1000/- (Silver Savings Account). The institution pays interest at 6% per annum on the savings account.



Account holder is required to maintain minimum
NORMAL SAVINGS ACCOUNT
Minimum Account Balance – ₹100/-
Rate of Interest – 6%
SILVER SAVINGS ACCOUNT
Minimum Balance – ₹1000/- Rate of Interest – 6% (Paid on monthly basis)
