About Us

With our unwavering faith and determination in the motto of “Where man is worthy than money”, we are moving ahead while writing the definition of progress by continuously moving forward on the path of progress. With the trust of respected members, account holders, agents, and employees, self-valuable guidance. Sahayog Multi State Credit Co-op. Society Ltd. has provided continuous service to its numerous members.

Every component of the society, mainly the middle and lower middle class, weaker sections, women and farmers, should get convenient and accessible banking services. We prioritise the security of their deposits, helping salaried businesswomen and farmers to understand their banking needs. It is our duty & goal to contribute to society’s economic and social development by providing schemes, accessible loans, projects, and facilities.

Understanding the importance of banking service in society, it is not just a commercial institution but also a social institution for social upliftment. Sahayog Multi State Credit Co-op. Society Ltd. will always remain committed to its duty and social loyalty and will try to give banking services to the last section of society.

Sahayog Multi-state Credit Co-operative Society was started with a vision to to provide convenient & accessible banking services to the rural part of India especially the middle and lower middle class, weaker sections, women and farmers. We prioritise the security of their deposits, helping salaried businesswomen and farmers to understand their banking needs. It is our duty & goal to contribute to society’s economic and social development by providing schemes, accessible loans, projects, and facilities.
